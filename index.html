Label-Me
Label-Me is a service which be able to groping people and labeling them depend on their health statuses. Through Label-Me we believe we can limit people activities and mobilities based on their label to get access to any public facilities and transportation. To avoid double-account problem for one user we use NIK (Nomor Induk Penduduk) to identify our user
Covid Hotspot
Covid Hotspot is a service to collect report data about health protocol violation in any public facilities by filling the report form and give a picture of the violation. The pecture the used to validate the report using machine learning model. After it validately valid it will collect for then share the data and calculate the risk of the corona virus spread in the reported area. If the report was too high in an area, the area will be marked as Hotspot and then we will send the info to the local user from their own mobile phone
Prokes Request
Prokes Request is a service to get your report about requesting health protocol availability to any public facilities or public transportation facilities like requesting a washstand availability or anything with a picture of the place you reported. Your report then will send to nearby covid prevention agency through their official email or forum they provide for any covid report
